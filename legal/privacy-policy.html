If you reach out through the contact form, I'll collect your name, email, and message — nothing more. That information is used only to get back to you. It's not shared, sold, or used for anything else.

This site uses two analytics tools to help me understand how people find and use it. Google Analytics is only active if you choose to allow analytics cookies. Netlify Web Analytics runs quietly in the background using general server data — no cookies, no individual tracking.

A few third-party services help keep the site running — things like hosting and the contact form. Some of these are based outside Canada, so your information may be processed there. I've chosen providers I trust, but I want you to know.

Please don't share anything urgent or crisis-related through the contact form — it's not monitored in real time. If you're in immediate distress, please reach out to a crisis line or emergency services.

Questions about this policy, or want to see or correct something I have about you? You can reach me through the contact page — I'm happy to help.
